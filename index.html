By students and for students

StuyDevs is a student-run organization that aims to provide a platform for students to learn and share their knowledge. We believe that students should be able to learn from each other and that this should be a community of like-minded individuals.

Mission Statement:

StuyDevs is a CS Bootcamp that teaches Computer Science fundamentals in Python/Java to students both inside and outside of Stuy.

A whole new world of opportunties

In itself Stuydevs will have semester-long projects and opportunities for independent student-mentor projects. Stuydevs will also have guest speakers from schools like NYU and Columbia to talk about advances in the tech field, garnering interest and opportunities for students to connect with professionals.
